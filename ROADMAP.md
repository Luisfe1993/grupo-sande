# Grupo Sande — Website Improvement Roadmap

## P0 — High Impact, Medium Effort ✅ IN PROGRESS
1. **Technical Blog** (`/blog`) — 6-8 SEO-optimized articles on industrial topics, establishing thought leadership and driving organic traffic
2. **Conversion Tracking** — Custom events on all CTAs and form submissions via Vercel Analytics
3. **Segment Landing Pages** — `/mineria` and `/construccion` with filtered products + relevant case studies
4. **PDF Quote Generation** — Cotizador outputs a branded PDF the buyer can share internally

## P1 — Medium Impact, Low Effort
5. **Lead Magnets** — Downloadable "Guía de Selección de Fijaciones" or "Manual de Levante Sincrónico" behind an email gate
6. **Social Proof Enhancement** — Add concrete numbers to testimonials, make them more prominent across pages
7. **WhatsApp Integration for M&A** — Direct confidential line on `/crecimiento` page
8. **Team Page Enhancement** — Photos, LinkedIn links, make leadership more approachable for M&A targets

## P2 — Strategic, Higher Effort
9. **Client Portal** — Login area for existing clients to track quotes, orders, reorder
10. **CRM Integration** — Pipe form submissions to a CRM (HubSpot free tier)
11. **Multi-language SEO** — Proper `hreflang` tags, separate meta descriptions per locale
12. **Video Content** — Actual project videos on the home page instead of placeholder section
